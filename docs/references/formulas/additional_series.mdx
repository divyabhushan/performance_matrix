## Syntax

`DSSERIES[seriesid]` - will reperesnt the respective sereis data

## Example

Conisider there is data in following structure 

| Parameter          | Series 1 | Series 2 |Series 3  |Series 4  |
|--------------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| US                 | 110    | 95     | 75     | 78     |
| India              | 85     | 70     | 100    | 80     |
| Germany            | 25     | 25     | 0      | 0      |

Here you can acces the Series 3 value of India using following formula
```javascript
SUM(DSSERIES3.India)
```
